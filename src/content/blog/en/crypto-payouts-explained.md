Once your rebate balance is available, you can withdraw it to any supported crypto network.
Here is what determines the speed and cost of a payout.

## Supported networks

- **TRC20 (Tron)** — the cheapest network fee, fastest confirmations.
- **ERC20 (Ethereum)** — widely supported, higher network fee.
- **BEP20 (BSC)** — low fees, good wallet compatibility.
- **SOL (Solana)** — fast and cheap, requires a Solana-compatible wallet.

## Choosing the right network

If you are unsure which network to pick, TRC20 is usually the safest default: low fees and
broad exchange support. Always double check that your receiving wallet supports the network
you select — sending to the wrong network can result in lost funds.

## Processing time

Most withdrawal requests are processed within a few hours. Once processed, the transaction
hash is available in your withdrawal history so you can track it on-chain.
