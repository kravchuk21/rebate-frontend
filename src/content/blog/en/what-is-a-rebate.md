A trading rebate is a small cashback you earn on every trade you make, paid out by the broker
or an intermediary partner. Instead of leaving that money on the table, you can have it
automatically calculated and paid to you in crypto.

## How it works

1. Connect your existing broker account.
2. Keep trading as you normally would.
3. Rebates accumulate daily based on your trading volume.
4. Withdraw your balance to a crypto wallet whenever you like.

## Why it matters

Rebates do not change your trading strategy and do not require any extra risk. They simply
return part of the spread or commission that already exists in every trade.

> The bigger your volume, the bigger the rebate — there is no cap.

If you already trade actively, connecting your account takes a few minutes and the payouts
start from your very next trading day.
