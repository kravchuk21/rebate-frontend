## About Bybit

Bybit is a global cryptocurrency exchange founded in 2018, serving millions of
traders worldwide. It offers spot trading, perpetual and futures contracts,
options, and a range of earn products — all backed by deep liquidity and a
high-performance matching engine built for active traders.

## Why trade Bybit through Sliceback

When you connect your Bybit account to Sliceback, you keep trading exactly as you
do today — same platform, same fees — while we share back a portion of the
trading commission you generate. Rebates are calculated daily and paid out in
crypto, straight to your wallet.

- **Daily payouts** — your rebate is credited every 24 hours, no waiting for
  month-end.
- **No extra cost** — connecting your account is free and never increases your
  trading fees.
- **Keep your account** — there is no need to open a new account or change how
  you trade.

## How to connect

1. Create your Bybit trading account (or use an existing one).
2. Go to **Broker Accounts** in your Sliceback dashboard and select Bybit.
3. Enter your Bybit account UID and submit it for review.

Once approved, your eligible trading volume is tracked automatically and your
rebate starts accruing daily.
